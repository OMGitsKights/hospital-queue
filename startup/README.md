## 🎯 Key Statistics to Remember

### **The Problem:**
- ⏰ **2-4 hours** average hospital wait time in India
- 💸 **₹15,000 crores** wasted annually in productivity loss
- 😞 **67%** of patients report frustration with unclear queue status

### **The Solution:**
- 💰 **₹0 capex** (no hardware needed)
- 🌍 **3 languages** (English, Hindi, Telugu)
- 🚨 **AI triage** (emergencies auto-prioritized)
- 💊 **Digital prescriptions** (never lose a prescription)

### **The Impact:**
- ⏱️ **60% reduction** in average wait time
- 📉 **40% decrease** in no-shows
- ⭐ **85%** patient satisfaction improvement

### **The Market:**
- 🏥 **25,000+ hospitals** in target market (Tier 1-2 cities)
- 💰 **₹500 crores** total addressable market
- 📈 **₹68 lakhs** Year 1 ARR target (100 hospitals)
